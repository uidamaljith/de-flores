Anisree Sreeraj is the founder and creative head of ‘de flores’ haute couture. An IT professional turned fashion designer took a plunge into the fashion entrepreneurship world, marking a significant presence in the world of fashion and customised dressmaking. After she quit her IT job, she debuted her career in the fashion industry with an eminent brand and dived into the formidable world of fashion by designing ensembles for various events and occasions. Later, she forayed into designing bespoke, customised bridal outfits and wedding attires. Her unstinted love and flair for fashion design, along with the wholehearted support of her family and friends, gave birth to the brand ‘de flores’.

For Anisree, designing is no less than an art, and she really enjoys making exquisite apparel out of any fabric. Anisree’s unique designs, watchful eyes for trends, and keen attention to detail are the driving force behind her competent craftsmanship. She has become a known name for blending unique elegance and style into her designs, especially in creating bridal outfits that are in vogue today. Anisree, the fashion lover and designer, exhibits designs that are an eclectic mix of customised fashion wear as well. Her couture speaks of royalty and class. She visions to rank ‘de flores’ as a fashion brand for women who want to experience a unique aesthetic in fashion, style and dressmaking
